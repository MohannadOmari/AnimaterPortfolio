CHARACTERS

Characters are what give you an attechment to movies, they reel you in like an anchor with emotions. They have to be well written in order to send the message you want to put out there. Some people can easily confuse an antagonist for a bad character when that is not entirely true; an antagonist that grinds your gears is a well written character that hus successfully played with your emotions. A bad character is a character that is not written well enough to loop you in to even care.
